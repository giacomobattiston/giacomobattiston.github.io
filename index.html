About

I am currently a Senior Research Officer at the ROCKWOOL Foundation in Berlin and a Research Associate of the Berlin School of Economics. My interests include political economy and labor economics, with a particular focus on the economics of migration. In my work, I apply structural econometric models to broad questions in political economy, and I use experimental methods to investigate the determinants of migration.
