About

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Economics at Bocconi University, Italy. My research focuses on migration economics. My aim is to understand the contradicting objectives of policy in managing this phenomenon, and their relation to the public and media attention to migration. I am also interested in the causes of human conflict.

My research exploits the interaction of different methods, ranging from structural econometrics to randomized controlled trials, and complemented with theory.
